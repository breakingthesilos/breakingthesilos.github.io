Breaking the Silos: Neurodiversity in Tech (open space)

Thursday 20 November 2025, 18:00 CET — Gumption, Kontich
It's not yet possible to sign up. Check back soon!

Breaking the Silos is hosting an event dedicated to Neurodiversity in Tech, continuing our mission to break siloed thinking and improve collaboration within Belgian organizations. This evening is a chance to collectively explore how we can build truly inclusive tech environments where neurodivergent individuals can contribute their full and unique potential.

Breaking the Silos: Neurodiversity in Tech (open space) is happening on Thursday 20 November 2025 as of 18:00 CET in the Gumption offices, Satenrozen 10, 2550 Kontich. Food and drinks will be available.

A collaborative open space event

Please note that this event will be in the dynamic open space format, not a traditional “mini-conference” with a fixed schedule. The content is created by you! Attendees are encouraged to propose topics such as inclusive hiring practices, building sensory-friendly workplaces, or the business case for neuroinclusion. The group will then collectively decide on the agenda and discussion groups. This format is designed for maximum collaboration and practical, shared learning.

We aim to organise an event that is as inclusive as possible. If you have any questions, suggestions or additional requirements, please provide them in the sign up form. Also, note that we enforce our code of conduct throughout the event and on our social channels.

Practicalities

  • Doors effectively open at 18:00, while the open space is set to start at 18:30.
  • Parking is available near the venue, including car charging stations.

We look forward to a stimulating and collaborative evening!
