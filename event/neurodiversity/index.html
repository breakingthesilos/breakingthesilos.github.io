Breaking the Silos: Neurodiversity

Tuesday 28 June 2022, 19:00 CEST — Teamleader, Ghent
This is a past event. It's no longer possible to RSVP.

Our first meetup focuses on Neurodiversity (ADHD, ASD, intellectual giftedness, etc.), featuring four live keynote speakers.

  • “ADHDeveloper” by Dietrich Moerman (founder of Bjièn bv, DataOps Ghent meetup and Breaking the Silos)
  • “Entrepreneurship in the spectrum” by Bruno Lowagie (founder of iText, Wil-Low bv, Screensaver bv, and QLT)
  • “The business case for neurodiversity” by Muriel Van Gompel (founder of Mindflow and Sailflow)
  • “Autism myths & misconceptions” by Magali De Reu (copywriter for tech and start-ups, founder of Autcasts)

Join us on Tue 28 June 2022, at 7 PM in the Teamleader offices in Ghent!

The entrance is free, and food and drinks will be available. Language of the talks: English.

Programme

  • 7:00 PM: doors, welcome with food & drinks
  • 7:30 PM: talks + small breaks in between
  • After the talks: connect and network

Thanks to all our organisers, Teamleader for hosting, and Bjièn for sponsoring this meetup.

Breaking the Silos organises mini-conferences on breaking siloed thinking and improving collaboration within Belgian tech companies. Our live events are organised all around Belgium. More about us…
